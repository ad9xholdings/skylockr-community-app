## Biology Background

Bulldog Red Ants are **real social insects** that form highly organized colonies. They represent:
- **Teamwork** — No ant works alone
- **Organization** — Every ant has a job
- **Communication** — Chemical signals coordinate actions
- **Resilience** — The colony survives even if individuals fall

> "Ants live in colonies and function as highly organized social insects, which naturally supports fast, repeated challenge gameplay." — Scientific Review

## Educational Content

### What is a Colony?

> A **colony** is a group of animals of the same species that live together, work together, and depend on each other. Ant colonies can have thousands or even millions of ants!

### Why Are Ants "Social"?

1. **Division of Labor** — Queens lay eggs, workers gather food, soldiers defend
2. **Communication** — Pheromones (chemical signals) share information
3. **Cooperation** — Ants work together to carry food, build nests, care for young
4. **Sacrifice** — Individual ants will die to protect the colony

### Ant Facts for Kids

- A single ant can carry 10-50 times its body weight
- Ants don't have ears — they "hear" through vibrations in the ground
- Some ant colonies live for 20+ years
- There are over 12,000 species of ants worldwide
- Ants are found on every continent except Antarctica

## Swarm AI Behavior

### Movement Patterns
1. **Scout Phase** — 1-2 ants appear first, test player reaction
2. **Gathering** — If scouts engaged, more ants arrive
3. **Swarm Charge** — Coordinated rush toward player position
4. **Retreat** — If 50%+ defeated, remaining ants scatter

### Response to Player
- **Correct answer:** One ant "defeated," removed from swarm
- **Wrong answer:** Ant advances closer
- **Timeout:** Ant attacks (reset challenge, no damage, just pressure)

## Ecological Connection

### Ants in Cotton Fields

Real cotton field ecosystems include ants because they:
- **Aerate soil** — Tunneling improves soil health
- **Control pests** — Some ants eat cotton-eating insects
- **Spread seeds** — Carry seeds while foraging
- **Food source** — Birds, lizards, and other animals eat ants

> "Using 'colony' as a vocabulary term is scientifically accurate and easy for players to connect to swarm behavior." — Educational Review
