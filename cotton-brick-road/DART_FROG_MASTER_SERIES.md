## 🔬 Scientific Framing

All frogs are **inspired by real Colombian poison dart frog species**, including:
- *Phyllobates terribilis* (Golden poison frog)
- *Dendrobates tinctorius* (Dyeing poison dart frog)
- *Oophaga pumilio* (Strawberry poison dart frog)
- *Adelphobates castaneoticus* (Brazilian poison frog)

Educational content reviewed for biological accuracy.
