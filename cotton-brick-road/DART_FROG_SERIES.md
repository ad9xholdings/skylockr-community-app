# 🐸 COLOMBIAN DART FROG SERIES - Cotton Brick Road

## Daily AR Invasion Challenge

### The Concept
As students progress along Cotton Brick Road toward Cotton Castle, the animals become increasingly realistic. A new threat has emerged: **Colombian Poison Dart Frogs** are invading the path!

### Game Mechanics
- **1 frog per day** appears in Augmented Reality
- **2-color combinations** - each frog features authentic dart frog coloration
- **Progressive realism** - frogs get more detailed/lifelike as you approach the castle
- **Educational hook** - learn about real amphibian biodiversity while mastering phonics

---

## DART FROG #001 - "Azure Striker"

### Appearance
| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Primary Color** | Electric Blue (vibrant cobalt) |
| **Secondary Color** | Jet Black |
| **Pattern** | Solid blue body with two thick black stripes running from nose to rear |
| **Realism Level** | 1/10 (cartoonish/cute - first encounter) |
| **Size** | Small (2 inches) |
| **Eyes** | Large, friendly, slightly cartoonish |

### AR Behavior
- Hops between lily pads on the path
- Makes a soft "ribbit" when approached
- **Challenge**: Tap the frog before it hops away to collect "Frog Token"
- **Reward**: +10 points, unlocks next phonics level hint

### Educational Fact
> "The blue poison dart frog is one of the most toxic animals on Earth. Indigenous Colombian tribes used their poison on blowgun darts for hunting!"

## Integration with Cotton Brick Road

### Location on Path
- Frogs appear randomly between levels
- Higher levels = more realistic frogs

### Gamification Elements
- **Frog Collection Book**: Track all 2-color combinations
