# 🦁 Lion Guardian Boss: Atlas the Field Protector

**Classification:** Guardian Class (Magical/Symbolic)  
**Location:** Southern Cotton Fields - Sunset Ridge  
**Unlocks:** After defeating Leo + Brick #50  
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐

---

## Lore

Atlas watches over the endless cotton fields at the edge of the road. He embodies the ecosystem itself — the balance between soil, sun, cotton plants, and all living things.

> *"The field gives life. The field takes life. Understand this balance, and you may pass."*

---

## 3-Phase Battle System

### 🟢 PHASE 1: TEACH (100% → 70% HP)

**Goal:** Learn ecosystem concepts  
**Duration:** ~30% of fight

#### Atlas's Behavior
| Move | Telegraph | Action |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Field Patrol** | Slow, deliberate pacing | Moves side to side, observes player |
| **Growth Stomp** | Paws plant firmly | Cotton plants briefly grow taller |
| **Sunlight Beam** | Mane catches light | Gentle beam illuminates challenge area |

#### Player Task
- **Pronounce:** "Ecosystem" (E-co-sys-tem)
- **Simple definition:** A community of living things interacting with their environment
- **Visual aid:** Cotton field diagram appears

#### Educational Content
> **Ecosystem:** All the living things (plants, animals, organisms) in a particular area, interacting with each other and their non-living environments (weather, earth, sun, soil, climate). A cotton field is an ecosystem!

---

### 🟡 PHASE 2: TWIST (70% → 35% HP)

**Transition:** Atlas walks to the road's edge, looks toward the horizon. Sunset light spreads across the cotton field. Visibility drops slightly as golden haze sets in.

> *"The sun sets. The night hunters wake. Do you still see clearly?"*

**Goal:** Apply knowledge under pressure  
**Duration:** ~35% of fight

#### Atlas's Behavior
| Move | Telegraph | Action |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Sunset Haze** | Sky darkens to orange | Reduced visibility, focus on telegraphs |
| **Root Trap** | Ground trembles near paws | Cotton roots briefly tangle one lane |
| **Pollen Burst** | Head shake, mane ruffle | Scatters visual particles |

#### Player Task
- **Pronounce:** "Environment" (En-vi-ron-ment)
- **Define:** What makes up an environment?
- **Identify:** Name 3 things in a cotton field ecosystem
- **Answer windows:** 7 seconds
- **Field awareness:** Must track Atlas while answering

#### Educational Content
> **Environment:** The natural world including living (biotic) and non-living (abiotic) things. In a cotton field: Biotic = cotton plants, insects, birds. Abiotic = soil, sunlight, water, air temperature.

---

### 🔴 PHASE 3: TEST (35% → 0% HP)

**Transition:** Atlas digs all four paws into the earth. Golden dust swirls, forming a protective ring around him. The road narrows into a "Field Protector's Zone."

> *"Prove you are part of this ecosystem, not separate from it!"*

**Goal:** Mastery of ecosystem relationships  
**Duration:** ~25% of fight

#### Atlas's Behavior
| Move | Telegraph | Action |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Protector's Circle** | Dust ring forms | Creates safe/unsafe zones on road |
| **Ecosystem Chain** | Sequential paw stamps | Multiple concepts in sequence |
| **Balance Beam** | Narrow path glows | Player must stay in safe zone while answering |

#### Player Task
- **Pronounce:** "Biodiversity" (Bi-o-di-ver-si-ty)
- **Define:** What is biodiversity?
- **Analyze:** Why is biodiversity important for ecosystems?
- **Apply:** How can we protect biodiversity in cotton fields?
- **Four-part challenge** (pronounce + define + analyze + apply)
- **Zone mechanic:** Must stay in safe area while answering
- **5-second windows** per part

#### Educational Content
> **Biodiversity:** The variety of all living things on Earth — different plants, animals, and microorganisms, plus the genetic differences within species. High biodiversity makes ecosystems stronger and more resilient. In a cotton field: bees (pollinators), ladybugs (pest control), birds (seed dispersal), and soil bacteria all create biodiversity!

### Sunset Haze (Phase 2+)
- Visibility reduced by 20%
- Telegraphs become MORE important

### Field Protector's Zone (Phase 3)

- Standing in unsafe zone = interrupted answer
- Must move + think simultaneously
