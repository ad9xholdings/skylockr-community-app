# 🦁 Lion Guardian Boss: Leo the Cotton King

**Classification:** Guardian Class (Magical/Symbolic)  
**Location:** Cotton Brick Road - Midway Checkpoint  
**Unlocks:** After Brick #25  
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐

---

## Lore

Leo is not a native Colombian animal. He is a **magical guardian** created by Mrs. Cotton's castle magic to test worthy travelers. He represents courage, leadership, and the protective spirit of the cotton fields.

> *"I am Leo, King of this Cotton Road. Prove your wisdom, or turn back."*

---

## 3-Phase Battle System

### 🟢 PHASE 1: TEACH (100% → 70% HP)

**Goal:** Learn the boss language  
**Duration:** ~30% of fight

#### Leo's Behavior
| Move | Telegraph | Action |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Royal Roar** | Mane glows gold, deep inhale | Single roar with shockwave |
| **Paw Swipe** | Paw scrapes ground | Wide swipe, slow telegraph |
| **Cotton Stomp** | One paw lifts high | Stomp creates dust cloud |

#### Player Task
- **Pronounce:** "Predator" (Pred-a-tor)
- **Simple definition:** An animal that hunts other animals
- **Long answer windows** (10 seconds)
- **Single phonics word** per prompt

#### Educational Content
> **Predator:** An animal that naturally preys on others. Lions are apex predators in their ecosystems. The word comes from Latin "praedari" meaning "to seize as plunder."

---

### 🟡 PHASE 2: TWIST (70% → 35% HP)

**Transition:** Leo slams his paw into cotton bricks, sending white fibers into the air. Deep roar shakes screen. Cotton fibers cling to his mane.

> *"Good. But can you keep up?"*

**Goal:** Add pressure, combine learned moves  
**Duration:** ~35% of fight

#### Leo's Behavior
| Move | Telegraph | Action |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Roar + Lane Block** | Mane glow + ground rumble | Roar knocks back, blocks one lane |
| **Double Swipe** | Both paws scrape | Two quick swipes in sequence |
| **Feint Pounce** | Crouches low, eye flash | Fake pounce, tests player reaction |

#### Player Task
- **Pronounce:** "Carnivore" (Car-ni-vore)
- **Define:** What is a carnivore?
- **Answer:** An animal that eats meat
- **Two-part prompts** (pronounce + define)
- **Shorter windows** (7 seconds)

#### Educational Content
> **Carnivore:** An animal that feeds on flesh. Lions are obligate carnivores — they must eat meat to survive. Their teeth and digestive systems are specialized for hunting.

---

### 🔴 PHASE 3: TEST (35% → 0% HP)

**Transition:** Leo rises tall, cotton fibers crown his mane like a royal headdress. Sky warms to sunset gold behind him.

> *"You have learned well. Now prove your MASTERY!"*

**Goal:** Mastery check  
**Duration:** ~25% of fight

#### Leo's Behavior
| Move | Telegraph | Action |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Cotton Crown Charge** | Full mane glow + paw stamp | Signature: Roar → Pounce → Swipe combo |
| **Chain Combo** | Rapid stance shifts | Three moves in quick succession |
| **Royal Judgment** | Eyes flash bright gold | Timed vocabulary sequence |

#### Player Task
- **Pronounce:** "Mammal" (Mam-mal)
- **Define:** What makes an animal a mammal?
- **Classify:** Is a lion a mammal? Why?
- **Three-step prompt** (pronounce + define + classify)
- **Short windows** (5 seconds)
- **Bonus stagger** for perfect answers

#### Educational Content
> **Mammal:** A warm-blooded vertebrate animal with hair/fur, that feeds milk to its young, and (usually) gives birth to live babies. Lions are mammals — they have fur, nurse their cubs, and are warm-blooded.

## Victory

**Leo bows his head, cotton crown dissolves into sparkles.**

> *"You have the heart of a king. Pass, young traveler. Mrs. Cotton awaits."*

- Castle Key Fragment #1 (if first guardian defeat)
