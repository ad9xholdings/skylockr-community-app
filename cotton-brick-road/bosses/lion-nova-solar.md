# 🦁 Lion Guardian Boss: Nova the Solar Lion

**Classification:** Guardian Class (Magical/Symbolic)  
**Location:** Cotton Castle Gates - Solar Plateau  
**Unlocks:** After defeating Atlas + Brick #75  
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

---

## Lore

Nova is the final guardian before Cotton Castle. She embodies the sun itself — source of all energy, giver of life, ultimate power. Only those who have mastered all lessons may face her.

> *"I am Nova. I am the first light of dawn and the last light of dusk. Show me you understand the circle of life."*

---

## 3-Phase Battle System

### 🟢 PHASE 1: TEACH (100% → 70% HP)

**Goal:** Learn energy and life cycle concepts  
**Duration:** ~30% of fight

#### Nova's Behavior
| Move | Telegraph | Action |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Sunrise Flash** | Mane flares bright | Brief light burst, no damage |
| **Solar Beam** | Eyes glow white | Focused light beam challenge area |
| **Dawn's Grace** | Gentle stance | Buffs player with light (positive) |

#### Player Task
- **Pronounce:** "Photosynthesis" (Fo-to-syn-the-sis)
- **Simple definition:** How plants make food from sunlight
- **Visual aid:** Sun → Leaf → Sugar diagram

#### Educational Content
> **Photosynthesis:** The process plants use to convert sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide into glucose (sugar) and oxygen. The word means "putting together with light." Cotton plants use photosynthesis to grow!

---

### 🟡 PHASE 2: TWIST (70% → 35% HP)

**Transition:** Nova's mane flares brighter than before. Screen catches a brief sunrise flash. Glowing tracks appear on the Cotton Brick Road. Music intensifies.

> *"The sun rises higher. The heat intensifies. Can you endure?"*

**Goal:** Apply under pressure, chained concepts  
**Duration:** ~35% of fight

#### Nova's Behavior
| Move | Telegraph | Action |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Chained Flashes** | Rapid mane pulses | Multiple light bursts, color-coded |
| **Solar Track** | Glowing paw prints | Marks safe path on road |
| **Heat Haze** | Air distortion effect | Slight visual warp, faster tempo |

#### Player Task
- **Pronounce:** "Energy Transfer" (En-er-gy Trans-fer)
- **Define:** How does energy move through an ecosystem?
- **Trace:** Follow the solar track while answering
- **Color matching:** Each flash = different challenge type
  - Gold flash = Pronunciation
  - Orange flash = Definition
  - Red flash = Classification
- **7-second windows**

#### Educational Content
> **Energy Transfer:** Energy flows through ecosystems from the sun → producers (plants) → consumers (herbivores) → predators (carnivores). When a lion eats a deer, energy transfers from deer to lion. When plants photosynthesize, they capture solar energy!

---

### 🔴 PHASE 3: TEST (35% → 0% HP)

**Transition:** Nova leaps skyward. The arena pauses — total silence. Then a burst of solar light explodes at center road. Nova lands in a pillar of golden fire.

> *"This is SOLAR JUDGMENT. Show me your WISDOM!"*

**Goal:** Ultimate mastery — all concepts combined  
**Duration:** ~25% of fight

#### Nova's Behavior
| Move | Telegraph | Action |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Solar Judgment** | Skyward leap → light explosion | Signature move — ultimate challenge |
| **Celestial Combo** | Sequential color bursts | All challenge types in sequence |
| **Final Stand** | Under 15% HP | Desperation speed boost, noble roars |
| **Solar Crown** | Full body glow | Brief vulnerability after special |

#### Player Task
- **Pronounce:** "Conservation" (Con-ser-va-tion)
- **Define:** What does conservation mean?
- **Apply:** How can we conserve ecosystems like cotton fields?
- **Synthesize:** Connect ALL previous concepts:
  - Photosynthesis → Energy Transfer → Biodiversity → Conservation
- **Five-part challenge** (pronounce + define + apply + synthesize + reflect)
- **5-second windows** per part
- **Bonus stagger:** Correct answers during Solar Crown = massive damage

#### Educational Content
> **Conservation:** Protecting and preserving natural environments, ecosystems, and wildlife so future generations can enjoy them. Conservation includes: protecting habitats, saving endangered species, reducing pollution, and sustainable farming. Without conservation, ecosystems lose biodiversity and collapse.

## The Solar Judgment Sequence

Phase 3's signature move tests everything:

1. **Leap** — Nova jumps skyward (3 second warning)
2. **Charge** — Light gathers above (2 seconds)
3. **EXPLOSION** — Solar Judgment hits center road
4. **Challenge Cascade** — 5 challenges in sequence:
   - Pronounce "Conservation"
   - Define conservation
   - Apply to cotton fields
   - Synthesize all concepts
   - Reflect: "Why does this matter?"
5. **Vulnerability Window** — If all correct, Nova staggers for 5 seconds
6. **Repeat** — Until defeated
