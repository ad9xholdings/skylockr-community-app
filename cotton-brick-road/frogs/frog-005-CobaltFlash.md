## Educational Fact

> **Color Flashing:** Some dart frog species can appear to "flash" different colors depending on lighting and viewing angle due to structural coloration in their skin. This is different from pigment — it's caused by microscopic structures that reflect light differently!

---

## Biology Note
Blue + yellow is a rare but striking combination in dart frogs. It mimics the colors of certain toxic beetles that frogs eat — the frogs actually absorb toxins from their diet and become poisonous themselves!

---

## Color Meaning
• **Royal Blue:** Primary body (structural color)  
• **Yellow:** Accent flashes (warning enhancement)
