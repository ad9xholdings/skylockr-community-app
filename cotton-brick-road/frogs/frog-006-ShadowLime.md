## Educational Fact

> **Forest Layers:** Dart frogs live in different rainforest layers — some on the forest floor, others in the canopy. The lime green color helps frogs blend into young leaves and vines in the understory layer, where sunlight creates dappled shadows.

---

## Biology Note
Juvenile dart frogs are often more brightly colored than adults. As they age, their colors may darken or shift — a phenomenon scientists are still studying. Each stage of life has different survival needs!
