## Educational Content

### Vocabulary: Biodiversity

> **Biodiversity** is the variety of all living things on Earth — different plants, animals, and microorganisms, plus the genetic differences within species.

### Why It Matters

Cotton fields with high biodiversity (bees, frogs, ants, birds, butterflies) are:
- **Healthier** — Natural pest control
- **More productive** — Better pollination
- **Resilient** — Survive weather/disease better

### Dart Frog Connection

Poison dart frogs are **biodiversity indicators**. Where they thrive, the ecosystem is healthy. Their bright colors warn predators: "This jungle is full of life!"

---

## Biology Fact

The emerald green color comes from **structural coloration** — microscopic structures in the skin reflect light, rather than pigments. This is the same way peacock feathers and butterfly wings create their colors!
