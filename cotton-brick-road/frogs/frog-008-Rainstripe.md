## Educational Fact

> **Striped Patterns:** Stripes are common in dart frogs and serve multiple purposes — they can break up the frog's outline (camouflage) OR emphasize it (warning). The sky blue + black combo is seen in high-altitude cloud forest species where visibility differs from lowland rainforest.

---

## Biology Note
High-altitude dart frogs often have more blue coloration due to different light conditions in cloud forests. The stripes may also help with thermoregulation — absorbing or reflecting sunlight depending on temperature needs.
