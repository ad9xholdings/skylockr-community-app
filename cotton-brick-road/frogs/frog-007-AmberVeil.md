## Educational Fact

> **UV Vision:** Many animals, including birds (main dart frog predators), can see ultraviolet light. Some dart frogs have patterns visible only in UV that warn predators even more strongly. The "veil" concept represents these hidden visual signals!

---

## Biology Note
Scientists use UV photography to discover patterns on frogs invisible to human eyes. These frogs are communicating in a spectrum we can't see — an entire hidden language of warning!
