## Educational Content

### Vocabulary: Habitat

> A **habitat** is more than just a place. It's everything an animal needs to survive: food, water, shelter, air, and space.

### Dart Frog Habitat

Where do poison dart frogs live?

| Need | Solution |
|------|----------|
| **Moisture** | Tropical rainforests near streams |
| **Food** | Small insects, ants, termites |
| **Shelter** | Under leaves, in hollow logs |
| **Safety** | Hidden spots to avoid predators |
| **Mates** | Near water for egg-laying |

### Habitat Loss

Poison dart frogs are endangered because their habitats are being destroyed by:
- Deforestation
- Climate change
- Pollution
- Pet trade capture

> Protecting habitats protects entire species!

## Biology Fact

The "glowing" stripes on Cobalt Phantom aren't actually glowing — they use **structural coloration** to reflect blue light so efficiently that they appear to glow in shady conditions. This is called **iridescence**!

## Stealth Challenge

### How to Find Cobalt Phantom

1. **Stop running** — Walk slowly
2. **Listen** — Watch for the faint hum
3. **Look at shadows** — It hides in shade
4. **Be patient** — It takes 5 seconds to appear
5. **Answer carefully** — Wrong answers make it fade

### Why This Matters

Real scientists studying dart frogs must be **patient and quiet** too! Sudden movements scare wildlife. The best naturalists move slowly and observe carefully.
