## Educational Fact

> **Dot Patterns:** Many dart frogs have distinctive spot or dot patterns that are unique to each individual — like fingerprints! Scientists use these patterns to identify and track frogs in the wild. The orange and black combination is common in Amazon basin species.

---

## Biology Note
The bright orange coloration is another example of aposematism — advertising toxicity. Predators learn quickly that orange + black = bad meal.

---

## Color Meaning
• **Orange:** Primary body (warning signal)  
• **Black:** Dot patterns (individual identification)
