## Educational Fact

> **Warning Coloration (Aposematism):** Bright yellow and black patterns signal toxicity to predators. This frog's colors are **inspired by warning coloration seen in species such as *Phyllobates terribilis***, the golden poison frog — one of the most toxic animals on Earth. Indigenous Emberá people of Colombia used its poison on blowgun darts for hunting.

---

## Biology Note
*Phyllobates terribilis* contains enough batrachotoxin to kill 10-20 humans. The bright yellow color says "don't eat me!" in a language every jungle predator understands.

---

## Color Meaning
• **Golden Yellow:** Primary body (warning signal)  
• **Soft Black:** Accent markings (contrast enhancement)
