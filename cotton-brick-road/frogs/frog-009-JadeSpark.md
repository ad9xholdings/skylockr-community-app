## Educational Fact

> **Species Classification:** Dart frogs were once all classified together, but DNA analysis revealed they're actually multiple unrelated groups that evolved similar traits independently! This is called **convergent evolution** — nature's way of finding the same solution (toxicity + warning colors) multiple times.

---

## Biology Note
The emerald green + yellow combination is classic "poison frog colors" but appears in species from different families. Nature doesn't care about our classification systems — if bright colors + toxicity works, evolution will find it again and again!
